Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tattoo appointment?

Fill out our contact form with your tattoo idea and reference images. We'll respond within 24 hours with availability and pricing. A $100 deposit secures your appointment.

What's the process for custom artwork commission?

Contact us with details about your project. We'll provide a quote and timeline. Upon agreement, you'll receive progress updates and have 2 rounds of revisions included.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We work by appointment only to ensure quality and attention to detail. Please contact us in advance to schedule your session.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments (PayPal, Venmo, etc.). A deposit is required to secure bookings.

How long is a typical tattoo session?

Sessions range from 1-4 hours depending on size and complexity. Larger pieces may require multiple sessions. We'll discuss timing during your consultation.

Can I request changes to a design?

Absolutely! We include 2-3 revision rounds in our design process. Additional revisions may incur a small fee.

Do you ship prints internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. See our catalog for details.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations 48+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit the deposit.
